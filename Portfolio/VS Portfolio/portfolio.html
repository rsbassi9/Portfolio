About me

As a recent graduate from the University of British Columbia and a self-taught front-end developer, I have always been fascinated by the intersection of creativity and logic. My interest in web design began as a hobby, but it quickly became clear that it was the perfect outlet for my passions. By combining my creative side with my technical skills, I have been able to build websites that not only look great but are also intuitive and user-friendly.

More About Me

While my background is primarily in using platforms like Wix to build websites, I am now eager to expand my knowledge of web development by learning more about languages and website customization. I am particularly interested in the emerging field of Web3 and the possibilities it presents for creating truly interactive and engaging online experiences. Ultimately, my goal is to use my skills to design and build digital products that people will love to use and enjoy. As an engineer who is fluent in Python, I bring a unique perspective to web design that is informed by my background in problem-solving and data analysis. I am excited to continue honing my skills in website design and development and to leverage my creativity and technical expertise to build truly exceptional digital products.
